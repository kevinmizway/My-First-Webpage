My favorite animal.

Red Panda!

Habitat

  • Lives in Western Asia.
  • Found between altitudes between 7,200 and 15,700 feet.
  • Prefers mountainous mixed decidous and conifer forests, especially with old trees and dense understories of bamboo.

      • Diet

      • Diet consists almost entirely of bamboo leaves.
      • Minor diet treats include fruit, flowers and the occasional egg or bird.
      • Red Pandas are attracted to artifical sugar making them unique among non-primates.
